About Viola Plants

Viola is a genus of flowering plants in the violet family Violaceae. They are typically small, herbaceous plants, and are often cultivated for their charming flowers.

Types of Viola

Common Viola

The common viola, also known as wild violet, is native to Europe and Asia, and it's often found in woodlands and shady areas.

Pansy

Pansies are a popular hybrid within the viola genus, known for their large, showy flowers in various colors.
