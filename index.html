About Viola Plants

Viola is a genus of flowering plants in the violet family Violaceae. They are typically small, herbaceous plants, and are often cultivated for their charming flowers.

Violets are a beautiful and delicate flower that come in a variety of colors, including blue, purple, white, and even yellow. There are over 600 species of violets, and they can be found all over the world, although most are native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

Violets are a popular choice for gardens because they are easy to care for and they bloom in early spring. They also have a sweet fragrance that is often used in perfumes and candles.
